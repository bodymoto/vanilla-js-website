Fundamentals are everything. Yet there are essential fundamentals to the software industry that go undiscussed. Try Googling, "the fundamentals of programming," and you'll find things like; analysis, or variables, or data structures, or algorithms. Each of these useful and compelling subjects, but something much deeper – and arguably more fundamental – is missing.

Before you lay down the concrete to support your building you must first understand the earth that lay beneath your feet. I have now refocused these principles into the new and exciting field of software development. It helps being a nerd, but I know that fundamentally this isn't a sprint – it's a marathon!
